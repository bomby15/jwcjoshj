About Me

JWC is a wood working bussiness, located in Raymondville, New York. I offer pick up or local delivery. I also go to craft fairs and try to promote my products. I make a lot of different products all the way from little flags to tables.

  • Thin Blue Flags
  • Thin Red Flags

  • Harley Davison Flags

  • Tables
  • Camp Signs
  • Much More
